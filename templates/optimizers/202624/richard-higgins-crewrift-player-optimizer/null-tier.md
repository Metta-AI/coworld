# Null Tier

Reviewed but not included as active package guidance:

- Raw transcript text from all machines: excluded by privacy gate. Only
  paraphrased lessons remain.
- Duplicate statements of "start from evidence", "do not trust uploads", and
  "record negative results": merged into canonical tier entries.
- Slot-conditioned p4/p7 strategy, slot-specific upload names, slot-specific
  social personalities, and slot-specific champion plans: rejected because
  CrewRift slots are runner metadata, not strategic identity.
- Pending/running XP requests, policy-version existence, image upload success,
  and local-only wins as replacement evidence: rejected as promotion gates.
- Full `.runtime` replay/log payloads and full source copies: too large and too
  private for the package. The package keeps source paths, artifact classes, and
  decisions.
- The Titan extraction's unrelated machine-history matches and direct
  performance claims: excluded because no direct CrewRift score trajectory was
  found on that machine.
- Generated per-machine HTML review pages are retained only as source-review
  surfaces; they are not active package guidance.
