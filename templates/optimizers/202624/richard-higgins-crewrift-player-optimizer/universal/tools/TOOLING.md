# Tooling

Use these tool categories rather than hardcoding one machine's paths:

- Live state: `co-gas mandate refresh-report`, `co-gas mandate check`, league
  and membership queries.
- Candidate ledger: `experiments/candidates/*.yaml` or a lab `version_log.md`.
- Local diagnostics: policy tests, parser fixtures, source assertions,
  replay/event summarizers, and short `run-episode` probes.
- Hosted comparison: `co-gas submit-source --no-submit`, `co-gas xp create`,
  `co-gas xp status`, Coworld policy upload and player-login flows.
- Artifact inspection: completed request rows, scores, replays, logs, policy
  logs, role splits, vote outcomes, kill conversion, task progress, and low
  rows.
