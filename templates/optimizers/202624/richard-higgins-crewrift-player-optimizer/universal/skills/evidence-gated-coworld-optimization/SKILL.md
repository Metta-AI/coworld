# Evidence-Gated Coworld Optimization

1. Confirm the target player identity, policy lane, and current baseline.
2. Collect completed artifacts or a focused local reproduction.
3. Name one failed capability.
4. Patch the smallest source owner.
5. Add the narrowest regression or diagnostic check.
6. Record candidate state and evidence.
7. Upload or request hosted comparison only after local evidence is credible.
8. Inspect completed hosted artifacts.
9. Promote, hold, or reject explicitly.
